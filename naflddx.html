NAFLD Fibrosis Score Calculator

Non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD) is the build up of extra fat in liver cells that is not caused by alcohol. In severe cases, a fatty liver may cause scarring (cirrhosis) over time and may even lead to liver cancer or liver failure. The NAFLD fibrosis score, developed by Angulo et al. (see below) is a simple scoring system that identifies advanced fibrosis without the need for a liver biopsy.
